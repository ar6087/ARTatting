About AR Tatting

Austin Reusch, the heart of AR Tatting, is a seasoned tattoo artist with five years of mastery in wildlife realism and blackwork. Based in Mukwonago, WI, Austin’s passion for capturing nature’s raw beauty shines through in every piece. Driven to push his craft further, he collaborates with top shops across Wisconsin, broadening his portfolio and bringing fresh inspiration to his clients.
